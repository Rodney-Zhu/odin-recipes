Bacon Recipe

Finished Bacon!

These are the instructions on how to cook bacon with the oven in Plymouth

Ingredients

  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Turn the oven on and set to 400 degrees (. Do not wait for the oven to preheat).
  2. Line bacon directly on a pan without the pieces touching each other
  3. Go ahead and place the pan in the oven.
  4. Cook the bacon for 20 minutes.
  5. Check every 5 minutes until bacon is cwispy (Nothing worse than burnt bacon!)
