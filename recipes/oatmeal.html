Oatmeal Recipe

These are the instructions on how to heat up oatmeal in Plymouth

Ingredients

  • quaker oats
  • milk (or substitute)
  • banana
  • raisins, berries, etc.
  • honey

Steps

  1. add oatmeal to a microwaveable bowl and cover with enough liquid, either water or milk
  2. set microwave power to 5 and cook for 2 minutes
  3. stir the oatmeal and then set the power to 5 again and cook for 2 minutes
  4. cut up the banana and add it along with the other toppings and honey to the finished oatmeal
