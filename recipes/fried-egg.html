Fried Egg Recipe

These are instructions on how to fry an egg in an aluminum pan

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • egg

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan on high temp until you see smoke
  2. Turn down the heat to 2 and add a generous amount of vegetable oil
  3. swish the oil around until the whole pan is covered
  4. crack the egg into the frying pan
  5. add oil to the pan in the sides where there doesn't seem to be enough oil
  6. keep lifting the pan until all the edges of the eggs turn cwipsy brown
  7. life edges delicately until the egg comes up on its own
  8. once the egg unsticks by itself, flip the egg and turn off the heat
  9. turn off the heat and let the egg cook a little longer on the residual heat
  10. serve the egg
